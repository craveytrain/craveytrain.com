Foundations · Motion

Three durations. Browser-default easing. No custom cubic-beziers, no bounces, no scroll effects.

0.2s — color, opacity, border-color on hover
0.3s — tag-cloud max-height expansion
0.4s — hero portrait grayscale → color

Section link

.section-link (main.css)
The one blessed "continue / view more / previous" affordance. Block-level, lives inside a .section-grid content column as its closer. Uses border-bottom (not text-decoration) for real space between text and line — same treatment as .hero-links a. Never preceded by a <hr>. Replaces the retired .view-all and .archive-link patterns.
